I am a 3rd year PhD student in Extragalactic Astronomy at the University of Western Ontario, in Canada. I am co-supervised by professors Pauline Barmby (**specialities:** *nearby galaxies, astroinformatics*) and Sarah Gallagher (**specialities:** *black holes, quasars, active galactic nuclei*). I study the variability of active galactic nuclei by investigating the variability timescale - black hole mass relationship with optical spectra and light curves, on a statistical scale.

You can find my publications and my CV on this website. If you want to reach out to me, please send me an email to ahelias@uwo.ca.
