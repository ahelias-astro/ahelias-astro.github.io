I am a 2nd year PhD student in Extragalactic Astronomy at the University of Western Ontario (or Western, for short), in Canada. I am co-supervised by professors Pauline Barmby (**specialities:** *nearby galaxies, astroinformatics*) and Sarah Gallagher (**specialities:** *black holes, quasars, active galactic nuclei*). I am currently working on low-mass black holes in galaxies, by trying to find a sample of such objects in some of the numerous sky surveys of the universe.

You can find my publications and my CV on this website. If you want to reach out to me, please send me an email to ahelias@uwo.ca.
