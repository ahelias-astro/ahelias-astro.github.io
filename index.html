Welcome to my website!

I am a 3rd year PhD student in Extragalactic Astronomy at the University of Western Ontario, in Canada.
I am co-supervised by Prof. Pauline Barmby and Prof. Sarah Gallagher. I study the variability of
active galactic nuclei by investigating the variability timescale - black hole mass relationship
with optical spectra and light curves.
