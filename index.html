Welcome to my website!

I am a 3rd year PhD student in Extragalactic Astronomy at the University of Western Ontario, in Canada.
I am co-supervised by Prof. Pauline Barmby and Prof. Sarah Gallagher. My PhD research aims to identify a
large sample of variability-selected low-mass active galactic nuclei, and to study their host galaxies
in order to better understand the evolution of the first black holes and galaxies in the universe.
